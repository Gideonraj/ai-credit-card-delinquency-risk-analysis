# AI-Powered Credit Card Delinquency Risk Analysis and Collections Strategy

## Overview

This project focuses on reducing credit card delinquency rates through AI-powered data analytics and decision-making frameworks. The objective is to identify customers at risk of delinquency, develop predictive risk assessment approaches, and recommend effective intervention strategies for collections teams.

The project applies Generative AI (GenAI), data analytics, predictive modeling concepts, and responsible AI principles to support informed financial decision-making.

---

## Problem Statement

Financial institutions face significant challenges due to increasing credit card delinquency rates. Early identification of high-risk customers enables proactive intervention, improves recovery rates, and reduces financial losses.

This project aims to:

- Analyze customer data to identify delinquency risk indicators.
- Assess data quality and uncover meaningful insights.
- Design a predictive framework for risk classification.
- Develop AI-driven collection and intervention strategies.
- Ensure recommendations are ethical, explainable, and compliant.

---

## Objectives

- Perform Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on customer datasets.
- Identify behavioral and financial risk indicators.
- Develop a no-code predictive modeling framework.
- Recommend personalized intervention strategies.
- Design an AI-powered collections workflow.
- Apply Responsible AI and regulatory compliance principles.

---

## Methodology

### 1. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

- Evaluated data quality and completeness.
- Identified patterns associated with delinquency.
- Analyzed customer risk indicators.
- Generated structured insights using GenAI-assisted analysis.

### 2. Delinquency Risk Assessment Framework

- Defined key variables influencing customer repayment behavior.
- Categorized customers into risk levels.
- Designed a predictive scoring framework.
- Established evaluation criteria for model performance.

### 3. AI-Driven Collections Strategy

- Proposed automated customer engagement workflows.
- Recommended intervention strategies based on risk categories.
- Designed scalable AI-powered collection processes.
- Leveraged agentic AI concepts for decision support.

### 4. Responsible AI Framework

- Incorporated transparency and explainability.
- Considered fairness and bias mitigation.
- Addressed privacy and compliance requirements.
- Promoted ethical AI adoption in financial services.

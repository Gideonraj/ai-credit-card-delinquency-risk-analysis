# Responsible AI Framework

## Objective
Ensure AI solutions are ethical, transparent, and compliant.

## Principles

### Fairness
- Avoid biased decision-making.
- Treat customers consistently.

### Explainability
- Provide understandable recommendations.
- Support decision transparency.

### Privacy
- Protect customer information.
- Follow data protection regulations.

### Accountability
- Maintain human oversight.
- Monitor AI performance regularly.

## Compliance Considerations
- Financial regulations.
- Data privacy requirements.
- Responsible AI guidelines.

## Conclusion
Responsible AI strengthens trust while improving business outcomes.

# Delinquency Risk Assessment Framework

## Objective
Develop a framework for predicting customer delinquency risk.

## Risk Categories

### Low Risk
- Consistent payment history.
- Low utilization ratio.

### Medium Risk
- Occasional delayed payments.
- Moderate utilization levels.

### High Risk
- Multiple missed payments.
- High outstanding balances.
- Increasing debt trends.

## Proposed Predictive Logic

Risk Score =
Payment History +
Credit Utilization +
Outstanding Balance +
Account Activity

## Evaluation Criteria
- Accuracy
- Precision
- Recall
- Explainability

## Expected Outcome
Early identification of customers likely to become delinquent.

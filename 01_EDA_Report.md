# Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) Report

## Objective
Analyze customer data to identify indicators associated with credit card delinquency.

## Data Quality Assessment
- Checked for missing values.
- Reviewed data consistency.
- Evaluated completeness of customer records.

## Key Risk Indicators
- Late payment history.
- High credit utilization ratio.
- Increasing outstanding balances.
- Irregular payment behavior.
- Frequent missed payment deadlines.

## Insights
Customers with a history of delayed payments and high utilization rates showed higher delinquency risk.

## Recommendations
- Monitor high-utilization customers.
- Trigger early warning alerts for missed payments.
- Improve customer segmentation based on risk levels.
